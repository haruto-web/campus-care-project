### 2. Alert Priority System
**Location:** Alerts & Notifications page

**What's New:**
- 4 severity levels: Critical, High, Medium, Low
- Color-coded alerts (Red, Orange, Blue, Gray)
- Warning banner for urgent alerts
- Severity filter

**How to Use:**
- Check warning banner first for critical alerts
- Use severity filter to focus on urgent cases
- Look for color-coded borders (thicker = unread)
- Critical alerts require immediate attention

**Severity Meanings:**
- **Critical (Red):** Immediate action required
  - High-risk students
  - Students requesting help
  - Extreme stress levels (5/5)
- **High (Orange):** Urgent attention needed
  - 5+ missing assignments
  - Attendance < 60%
  - High severity teacher concerns
- **Medium (Blue):** Monitor closely
  - 3-4 missing assignments
  - Attendance 60-75%
  - Medium severity concerns
- **Low (Gray):** Routine follow-up
  - Low severity concerns

---

### 3. Year Level Filtering
**Location:** At-Risk Students & Interventions pages

**What's New:**
- Filter students by year level (1, 2, 3, 4)
- Combine with other filters

**How to Use:**
- Select year level from dropdown
- Combine with risk level or status filters
- Click "Filter" to apply
- Useful for year-specific programs

---

### 4. Age Range Analysis
**Location:** Reports & Analytics page

**What's New:**
- Identifies most problematic age range
- Bar chart showing high-risk students by age
- Age groups: 15-17, 18-20, 21-23, 24+

**How to Use:**
- Check "Age Range of High-Risk Students" section
- Note the warning banner showing most problematic age
- Use insights to design age-appropriate interventions
- Target resources to age groups with highest risk

## ðŸ” Filtering Tips

### At-Risk Students
1. **Start Broad:** View all high-risk students
2. **Narrow Down:** Add year level filter
3. **Search Specific:** Use name/email search
4. **Take Action:** Click "Create Intervention"

### Alerts
1. **Check Warning:** Look at banner first
2. **Filter by Severity:** Start with Critical
3. **Filter by Type:** Focus on specific issues
4. **Mark as Read:** Keep track of reviewed alerts
5. **Resolve:** Close alerts after taking action

### Interventions
1. **View Scheduled:** See upcoming sessions
2. **Filter by Year:** Focus on specific year level
3. **Update Status:** Mark as completed after sessions
4. **Add Outcomes:** Document results

## ðŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

**Charts not loading?**
- Check internet connection (Chart.js loads from CDN)
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache

**No data in age range chart?**
- Students need date_of_birth entered
- Ask admin to update student records

**Warning banner not showing?**
- Good news! No critical alerts
- Check if alerts are already resolved

**Filters not working?**
- Make sure to click "Filter" button
- Try clearing filters and reapplying
- Check if any students match criteria
